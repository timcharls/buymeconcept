Hunger Pains

The hunger is eating you alive, and all you want is that shiny new toy. It's yours and you can already imagine all the ways that you will play with it. After you've been told all of those beautiful promises, you know that you need this product. It has build a desire in your heart, and you want to fill a void that you just found out exists; It needs to be satiated.

Then come are voices in your head, and they are telling you to go get it. Can you afford it? Who cares! You "need" it, and that trumps all. They burn inside of you, screaming, and your mind begs for some relief. You know what you want, and your going to get it no matter what.

These are all symptoms of consumer hunger pains. They sneak up on on you when you least expect it. Flipping through the pages of wired you see the latest galaxy tablet has some rave reviews. Your friends all have one, but your the tech fanatic! What is wrong with the world? The balance of power has shifted. Hunger pains are triggered by the unconcious want of a new product. As the pains are get stronger, can you resist?

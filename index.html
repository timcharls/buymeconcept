An Interpretation of Barbara Kruger.

Consumerism today is a whole new level than it has been in the past. With the use of technology people have more immediacy in their buying power than they did in the eighties when Kruger was creating her anti advertising art. More and more products are being created to fill new "needs" in the consumer market.

While these products are exciting convincing on their own advertising plays a huge role in persuading people that they can't live with out these products. They help to fill voids in people's life. Rather they create voids in people's life, because before it was brought to their attention consumers hadn't realized the void was even.

In todays market new products and services are created each day and they all have a team of people who try to create a voice that speaks to consumers in the loudest and most unique way.

Sweet Nothings

Lies! Beautiful whispers. Sweet Nothings are all the enticing things that people wish to hear. This term is normally used in a romantic context. when someone is whispering sweet nothings in your ear, it usually a way of seduction. Advertising tells people things they want to hear in order to sell a product. They are romanticizing the product and telling you exactly what you want to hear. First they introduce this new glossy product, and catch your curiosity. Then they start to tell you how it will change you and your life. You want better skin! Here is a product that will make your skin flaw less. You want a vacuum that runs on it's own? Well here is one of those. Your life has a suddenly become that much easier when you purchased that item.

Whether it really works is not the point. It's the satisfaction you get from buying that item, because of everything you've been told it would do for you. The words are sugary. They are filled with promises, and you can't get enough. You've been hooked. Time to change your life.
